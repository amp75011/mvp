{{ _('Thai Curry with Chickpeas and Sweet Potato') }}

{{ _('Ingredients for 4 Servings') }}

{{ _('• 2 large sweet potatoes, peeled and cubed') }}

{{ _('• 1 can (15 oz) of chickpeas, drained and rinsed') }}

{{ _('• 1 can (14 oz) coconut milk') }}

{{ _('• 2 tablespoons Thai red curry paste') }}

{{ _('• 1 onion, finely chopped') }}

{{ _('• 2 cloves garlic, minced') }}

{{ _('• 1 tablespoon fresh ginger, grated') }}

{{ _('• 1 tablespoon cooking oil') }}

{{ _('• 1 red bell pepper, diced') }}

{{ _('• 1 cup vegetable broth or water') }}

{{ _('• Juice of 1 lime') }}

{{ _('• 1 tablespoon soy sauce or fish sauce (optional)') }}

{{ _('• Salt to taste') }}

{{ _('• Fresh cilantro leaves, for garnish') }}

{{ _('• Cooked rice or naan bread, for serving') }}

{{ _('Preparation') }}

{{ _('• Heat the oil in a pot over medium heat. Sauté onion until translucent. Add garlic and ginger, sautéing until aromatic.') }}

{{ _('• Stir in the curry paste and cook for 1-2 minutes. Pour in coconut milk and broth, and bring to a simmer.') }}

{{ _('• Add sweet potatoes and chickpeas. Simmer for 15-20 minutes or until sweet potatoes are tender.') }}

{{ _('• Add red bell pepper and cook for 5 more minutes. Stir in lime juice and soy sauce, and season with salt.') }}

{{ _('• Serve over rice, garnished with cilantro.') }}

{{ _('Grilled Salmon with Asparagus and Cherry Tomatoes') }}

{{ _('Ingredients for 2 Servings') }}

{{ _('• 1 salmon fillet (approximately 6-8 ounces)') }}

{{ _('• 1 bunch of asparagus, ends trimmed') }}

{{ _('• A handful of cherry tomatoes on the vine') }}

{{ _('• 2 tablespoons olive oil') }}

{{ _('• Salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste') }}

{{ _('• 1 lemon, cut into wedges') }}

{{ _('• Fresh thyme or dill for garnish') }}

{{ _('• Optional: Red pepper flakes') }}

{{ _('Preparation') }}

{{ _('• Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. Season the salmon with salt and pepper.') }}

{{ _('• Toss the asparagus and cherry tomatoes in olive oil, salt, and pepper, and place them on the grill.') }}

{{ _('• Grill the vegetables for 3-4 minutes, then add the salmon and grill for 5-6 minutes on each side.') }}

{{ _('• Serve the salmon on a bed of asparagus with tomatoes and a lemon wedge on the side.') }}

{{ _('• Garnish with fresh herbs before serving.') }}

{{ _('Grilled Trout with Vegetables and Figs') }}

{{ _('Ingredients for 2 Servings') }}

{{ _('• 2 trout fillets') }}

{{ _('• 1 cup broccoli florets') }}

{{ _('• 1 cup green beans, ends trimmed') }}

{{ _('• 1 cup sliced carrots') }}

{{ _('• 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved') }}

{{ _('• 4 fresh figs, quartered') }}

{{ _('• 2 tablespoons olive oil') }}

{{ _('• Salt and pepper to taste') }}

{{ _('• Optional herbs like thyme or rosemary') }}

{{ _('Preparation') }}

{{ _('• Preheat your grill pan over medium-high heat. Brush the trout fillets with olive oil and season with salt, pepper, and optional herbs.') }}

{{ _('• Grill the trout for about 4-5 minutes per side or until it flakes easily with a fork.') }}

{{ _('• Steam broccoli, green beans, and carrots until just tender. Add them to the grill pan with the tomatoes, drizzle with olive oil, and season.') }}

{{ _('• Grill the vegetables for 2-3 minutes, then add the fig quarters and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes.') }}

{{ _('• Serve the grilled trout and vegetables hot, with the figs adding a sweet contrast.') }}
